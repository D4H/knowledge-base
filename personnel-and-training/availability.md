# Availability

View a list of all [personnel](members/) and their availability on a specific date or time. For each person, youâ€™ll have their current location, if they are attending an activity, and the ability to see if all their [qualifications](qualifications/) are still valid on this date.

To view this information:

* Go to **Planning** > **Availability**
* You can change the date and time or filter the report by the **Group** drop down
* Click **Go** to see the updated information

{% hint style="info" %}
